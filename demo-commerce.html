  • Direct Purchase Quick Order with model + diopter selection in two clicks
  • Add-to-Consignment Quick Order for high-frequency repeat purchases
  • Preferred Products surface frequently purchased items based on rolling purchase history
  • Differentiated for Existing Customer Consignment — billing existing consignment lenses, bill-and-replace, and serial validation pre-checkout
Value: Reduces search friction, shortens time-to-order, and aligns commerce behavior with traceability requirements for implantable devices.

🔗 Differentiated

Consignment & Bill-and-Replace

Serial-number-centric consignment workflows: bill existing consignment lenses, execute bill-and-replace actions, and validate serials before checkout.

  • Pre-checkout serial validation calls prevent billing and inventory errors at source
  • Bill-and-replace aligned with traceability requirements for implantable devices
  • Lot recall awareness flags ineligible serials before they hit checkout
  • Goes significantly beyond baseline platform consignment support
Value: Aligns commerce with regulatory traceability — no more after-the-fact fulfillment corrections or unbilled implants.
🔄 Automation

Recurring Orders & Replenishment

Schedule automated replenishment at daily, weekly or monthly cadence with configurable start/end dates — including indefinite schedules.

  • Self-service schedule management from the user dashboard
  • Modify, add/remove items, discontinue or reactivate existing schedules
  • Confirmation flows for discontinuation prevent accidental cancellations
  • 3,891 recurring orders active across 34 markets · ▲ +23% YoY
Value: Reduces manual workload and stabilizes inventory replenishment across surgical facilities.

🤝 Teamwork

Collaborative Saved Carts

Public saved carts visible to all users sharing the same ship-to scope, enabling collaborative procurement and standardized ordering templates.

  • Extends baseline saved cart concepts with public/private behavior
  • Procurement coordinators & clinical staff share templates without duplicating order entry
  • Templates can be versioned, copied or promoted to recurring orders
  • Sharing scope is tied to ship-to — never wider than the operational unit
Value: Eliminates duplicated effort, encodes institutional best practice, and shortens onboarding for new clinical staff.
↩️ Accuracy

Returns & Post-Order Control

Multiple return initiation paths and post-order edit windows — addressing common clinical procurement errors without requiring support intervention.

  • Return by PO number, intuitive code, serial number or barcode scan
  • Order withdrawal within a defined window after placement
  • Edit recently-placed orders for wrong diopter, wrong ship-to, etc.
  • Reduces fulfillment-correction tickets and downstream billing errors
Value: Reduces support intervention, fulfillment errors and downstream billing rework — improving NPS and reducing operational cost.

📜 Digital Commerce

Digital Certificates & Device Assets

Purchase and download digital certificates for devices and treatment courses directly through the platform — digitizing activation workflows that are typically manual.

  • Customers select certificates by equipment model and complete purchase through the storefront
  • Self-service device management — add, update or remove devices
  • View purchased credits and active courses from a single page
  • Reduces time-to-activation for clinical devices and training certifications
Value: Digitizes manual activation, reduces time-to-activation for clinical devices, and gives customers self-service control of their licensed assets.
🔀 Supply Continuity

Product Substitution & Availability Engine

Replaces unavailable or discontinued products with clinically appropriate alternatives under defined reason codes. Smart suggestions maintain ordering continuity even when supply is constrained.

  • Some substitutions are seamless; others surface a transparency warning to the user
  • Defined reason codes ensure substitutions are explainable and auditable
  • Availability engine provides estimated delivery dates and inventory messages on low-stock items
  • Reduces abandoned carts and "where is my product?" support inquiries
Value: Maintains revenue continuity during supply disruptions while preserving clinical appropriateness and user transparency.

Embedded AI Assistant

An AI assistant embedded directly in the commerce experience — answering product queries, providing navigation guidance and assisting with order-related issues.

  • Answers product queries about catalog, eligibility, compatibility
  • Provides navigation guidance across the storefront, returns, certificates

AI Extensions on the Roadmap

Where Commerce is heading next.

Today's Embedded AI Assistant is just the start. The H2 2026 backlog adds four more AI features that move from helpful to proactive.

🎯

Advanced Search with Clinical Intent

Interprets queries like "Toric IOL for 2.5D astigmatism with blue light filter" rather than literal keyword matching — surfacing the right SKUs even when the user's vocabulary doesn't match yours.

⚙️

Compatibility Mapping

When a customer searches for an Active Sentry handpiece-class item, AI auto-surfaces the compatible side-car tips, sleeves and tubing packs — preventing missing-piece orders that delay surgeries.

📈

Predictive Case Load & Suggested Carts

Analyzes rolling buying patterns and upcoming surgical schedules to flag discrepancies on the current order — and pre-fills carts for missing items before the customer realizes there's a gap.

🧰

Virtual Pack Builder

A guided AI interface that helps customers customize Custom Procedure Trays — preventing clinical errors at point of sale by validating compatibility and completeness.
