Why Year 4 AI matured at exactly the right moment in this transformation

AI didn't arrive on a calendar — it became enterprise-ready over time. In Year 1 and Year 2 (2022–2024), AI was still nascent for clinical workflows: GenAI tooling was emerging, RAG patterns weren't production-mature, and agentic frameworks barely existed. Years 2 and 3 (Oct 2023 – Sep 2025) introduced the first-generation AI plays — basic chatbots, sentiment analytics, rules-based "self-healing" data constructs. The real shift happened in Year 4 (Oct 2025+) when GenAI tooling, MCP-style agent protocols, and AIOps matured simultaneously. That's why ~25 of the AI-driven items live in Year 4: AI is no longer a workstream — it's the operating fabric. Predictive surgery forecasting, GenAI-assisted code review, agentic deployment pipelines, AI-personalized training, AI-augmented PMO — every lane gets an AI accelerant in Year 4 because, finally, the technology was trustworthy enough to deploy at clinical scale.
