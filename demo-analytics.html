From dashboards to AI-enabled decisions.

🔬 Surgery Insights

Volume, growth & mix — benchmarked.

Track procedure volume over time and compare growth rate, volume and mix against industry benchmarks. Evaluate lens type and manufacturer preferences across associated practices.

  • Procedure volume tracking over rolling time windows (30d, 90d, 12mo)
  • Growth-rate, volume-mix, share-of-surgeries comparisons against industry benchmarks
  • Lens type & manufacturer preferences across all associated practices in a group
  • Drill-downs by surgeon, OR, region, procedure type
Value: Practice leaders see whether they're growing faster or slower than peers — and where the variance is concentrated.

⭐ Premium Tech Adoption

Adoption depth by surgeon, practice & region.

Measure share of surgeries using premium lenses and procedure technologies. Drill into adoption rates by surgeon, practice and region — all benchmarked against peers.

  • Premium share tracked across practices and surgeons
  • Per-surgeon coaching opportunities surfaced from adoption data
  • Regional variation visible at a glance — informs market strategy

💰 Revenue Insights

Surgery volume → revenue trends.

Translate surgery volume into estimated revenue trends using fee definitions managed inside the platform — supporting business planning conversations with practice leadership.

  • Fee definitions managed in-platform — admins configure rates by procedure, region, tier
  • No IT involvement for fee changes — leaders update directly
  • Procedure-type breakdown shows where revenue concentration is
  • Peer comparison on revenue-per-case highlights pricing & mix opportunities
Value: Bridges operational and financial conversations — practice leadership can translate surgery growth into a credible revenue picture in seconds.
📊 Benchmarking

National, regional, peer-group baselines.

Compare performance against national, regional and peer-group baselines with curated benchmark data and segmentation logic. Roadmap includes percentile breakdowns and product-type variations.

  • Curated benchmark data — not raw market scrapes; governed for accuracy

  • Peer groups based on case volume and practice type
  • Roadmap: percentile breakdowns & product-type variations for finer-grained context
Value: Practice leaders can answer "are we good?" with credible peer context — not gut feel.

"Where do we rank against peers?"
AI · Conversational Analytics: You're in the 72nd percentile overall vs. similar-volume peer practices. Strongest: premium tech adoption (78th percentile). Weakest: revenue per case (48th percentile) — driven by case mix. Lifting refractive share by ~3 pp would close most of that gap.

🤖 AI-Enabled

Conversational AI — Ask Me Anything.

An embedded AI assistant provides an "Ask Me Anything" interface. Start from predefined insight-oriented questions or type free-form natural language queries about practice performance.

  • Predefined insight-oriented questions reduce the cold-start problem
  • Free-form natural language queries about surgeons, regions, mix, revenue, peers
  • Early phases: queries matched to predefined insights and logged to inform new insight definitions
  • Later phases: GenAI-driven real-time analysis with follow-up Q&A capability
Value: Lowers the barrier from "I have a question" to "I have an answer" — administrators stop waiting on analysts and start exploring directly.
AI Extensions on the Roadmap

From insight to action.

Today's analytics surface what happened. The H2 2026 backlog adds AI capabilities that surface what's worth doing about it — opportunity, leakage, peer fit, churn risk.

🎯

Lost Opportunity Identification

Analyzes "white-space" between surgeries to identify optimization opportunities — moving beyond passive metrics into actionable, dollar-quantified gaps vs. peer norms.

👥

Dynamic Peer Grouping

Replaces static national averages with AI-clustered peers (e.g., high-volume private refractive practices) — making benchmarks fairer and more actionable.

💧

Revenue Leakage Detection

Flags spending pattern anomalies vs. peers — e.g., consumable rationalization opportunities or unbilled implants traced back through commerce data.

⚠️

Churn Prediction

Monitors account behavior and senses drops in activity to trigger Sales-Rep alerts before churn occurs — converting reactive retention into proactive engagement.
